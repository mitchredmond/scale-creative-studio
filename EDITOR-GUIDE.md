# Your Portfolio — Quick Start Guide

Welcome to your portfolio! This guide shows you how to add and edit your work.

---

## Logging In

Go to: **`your-project.sanity.studio`** (your developer will give you the exact link)

Log in with your email/Google/GitHub account.

---

## The Dashboard

When you log in, you'll see two things in the sidebar:

1. **Site Settings** — Your name, bio, photo, contact info
2. **Projects** — Your work

---

## Setting Up Your Profile (Do This First)

1. Click **Site Settings**
2. Fill in your info:
   - **Your Name**: How you want to be credited
   - **Your Title**: e.g., "Creative Director"
   - **Location**: e.g., "Los Angeles, CA"
   - **Bio**: A short paragraph about yourself
   - **Profile Photo**: Upload a nice headshot
   - **Contact Email**: Where people can reach you
   - **Social Links**: Add Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.
3. Click the green **Publish** button (top right)

---

## Adding a Project

1. Click **Projects** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+** button (top right)
3. Fill in the fields:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Title** | Project name (e.g., "Nike Summer Campaign") |
| **Slug** | Click "Generate" — this creates the URL |
| **Featured** | Check this for your best 1-2 projects |
| **Date** | When you completed it |
| **Client** | Who hired you |
| **Your Role** | What you did (e.g., "Creative Director") |
| **Short Description** | 1-2 sentences shown on the homepage |
| **Thumbnail** | Main image for the grid — use landscape format |
| **Tags** | Categories (e.g., Branding, Campaign, Digital) |
| **Project Images** | All the images, in the order you want them |
| **Live URL** | Link to the live project (if it exists online) |
| **Credits** | Credit your team (optional) |

4. Click **Publish**

The site updates automatically in about 30 seconds.

---

## Editing a Project

1. Click **Projects**
2. Click the project you want to edit
3. Make changes
4. Click **Publish**

---

## Deleting a Project

1. Click **Projects**
2. Click the project
3. Click the **⋮** menu (top right)
4. Select **Delete**
5. Confirm

---

## Image Tips

- **Thumbnail**: Use landscape images (wider than tall), at least 1200px wide
- **Project Images**: Upload high quality — they get optimized automatically
- **Full Width**: Check "Full Width" for hero images you want to span edge to edge
- **Captions**: Add captions if you want text under specific images

---

## Common Questions

**How long until changes appear?**
About 30 seconds after you click Publish.

**Can I preview before publishing?**
Yes! Changes are saved as drafts. Only "Publish" makes them live.

**Can I reorder projects?**
Projects sort by date (newest first). Change the date to reorder.

**Can I undo a change?**
Click the clock icon (top right) to see version history and restore previous versions.

**The site looks broken?**
Try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R). If that doesn't work, contact your developer.
